Pune–Mumbai cosmology and astro-particle meeting

Welcome to the fifth chapter of the Pune–Mumbai cosmology and astro-particle meeting. The meeting aims to harness the rich local expertise in these fields and to foster meaningful collaborations. This edition will feature in-depth discussions and focused parallel sessions, designed to encourage exchange of ideas and promote collaborative research, particularly supporting the growth and engagement of early-career researchers.

General Information

On Friday, we will meet in Room 101 on the ground floor of the Lecture Hall Complex (Building 1 on the map). Discussion rooms will be located either in the Lecture Hall Complex or in the Main Academic Building (Building 2 on the map).

Breakfast is available at the guest house, and other meals are available either at the Guest House or in the Dining Hall Complex (Building 5 on the map) on payment. Lunch will be arranged for all participants at the Guest House on both days.
